When you click the login button, you are redirected to a Spotify login page, where you can authenticate your account access. Spotify will then redirect you back to this site with an access token that can be used to retrieve your information. Since it is purely Spotify that handles authentication, all access codes and personal data exists only within your machine and the Spotify server, and do not go through the spotinfo server at any point. This also means that any computations or operations performed using your data will be made within your browser, the Spotinfo server simply provides the building blocks to perform these computations.
