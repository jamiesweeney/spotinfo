Spotinfo Home-page

Welcome

This is the home page of Spotinfo.

Below you can find some basic account details such as your username and product type. Use the navigation buttons in the header to visit the different pages.


There are currently 2 main features:

Playlists:

  • Provides a sortable table with playlists information for both owned and followed playlists.
  • A barchart and overlaid line graph, showing the distribution of playlist sizes.
  • Individual playlist pages with in-depth analysis.

Favourites:

  • Provides a sortable table with your favourite songs and artists, over 3 differnt time periods.
  • A network graph which shows the genre breakdown of all favourite artists.
